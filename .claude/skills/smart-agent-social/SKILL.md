# Social Content

**When to Use:** Write social media content (Twitter/X, LinkedIn, Facebook) for product launches, feature announcements, tips, or engagement. Use when promoting Smart Agent features or sharing updates.

## Purpose

Create platform-optimized social content that drives awareness and engagement.

## Platform Guidelines

### Twitter/X
- **Length**: 280 characters max
- **Style**: Punchy, hook in first line
- **Format**: Short sentences, emojis sparingly
- **CTA**: Link in bio or direct link

### LinkedIn
- **Length**: 1-3 paragraphs, up to 3000 characters
- **Style**: Professional, value-driven
- **Format**: Line breaks for readability, bullet points
- **CTA**: Comment/share/link

### Facebook
- **Length**: 1-2 paragraphs
- **Style**: Conversational, community-focused
- **Format**: Questions, relatable stories
- **CTA**: Engagement-focused

## Writing Tips

1. **Hook first** - First line must grab attention
2. **One idea per post** - Don't overload
3. **Show, don't tell** - Specific examples beat claims
4. **CTA always** - What should they do next?
5. **Hashtags sparingly** - 2-3 relevant ones on LinkedIn
